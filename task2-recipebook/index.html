Chicken Biryani

A classic Indian rice dish loved by millions

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made w ith basmati rice, tender chicken. and a blend of spices. This recipe serves 4-6 people and takes approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes to prepare and cook.

For the Chicken Masala:

500g Chicken (cut into pieces) 2 Onions (sliced thin)
    2 Tomatoes (chopped)
    1 cup Yogurt
    2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste 2 Green Chilies
    1 tsp Turmeric Powder
    2 tsp Red Chili Powder
    1 tsp Garam Masala
    Fresh Mint and Coriander Leaves 3 tbsp Oil or Ghee
    Salt to taste

For Garnishing:

        Fried Orions (Birista)

Step 1: Prepare the Rice

Wash the basmati rice 3-4 times until the water runs clear. Soak in water for 30 minutes, then drain. Boil 4 cups of water with bay leaves, cardamoin, cloves, and cinnamon. Add rice and cook until 7OoZo done. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: Cook the Chicken

Heat oil in a heavy-bottomed pan. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, sauté for 2 minutes. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.

Step 3: Add Spices

Add Oirineric. red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2 minutes. Add chicken pieces and cook on high heat for 1 minutes. Add yogurt and mix well. Cover and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes until chicken is tender.

Step 4: Layer the Biryani

In a large heavy pot, spread the chicken masala at the bottom. Layer the partially cooked rice on top. Sprinkle fried onions. mint leaves, coriander leaves. and saffron milk on top. Add a tablespoon of ghee. Cover tightly with a lid.

Step 5: Dunn Cooking (Steam)

Cook on high heat for 3-4 minutes, their reduce to the lowest heat and cook for 25-30 minutes. Do not open the lid during this process. Turn off heat and let it rest for 5 minutes.

Step 6: Serve

Gently mix the layers with a fork. Serve hot with raita, salau, or a simple onion salad. Garnish with fresh mint and fried onions.
